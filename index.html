Main Questions

What is the Bosnian Genocide?

The Bosnian Genocide mostly refers to large events that took place within the Bosnian War. The first being the Srebrenica massacre, that took place in July of 1995, that lead to the death of over 8,000 Bosniaks. The second refers to the other crimes against humanity and elements of ethnic cleansing that were carried out in Bosnian during the war.
